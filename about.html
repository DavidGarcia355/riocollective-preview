About The Rio Collective

Located inside IMAGE Studios at 460 W Chicago Ave, The Rio Collective brings together barbers who care about precision, consistency, and a relaxed studio vibe.

From tapers and skin fades to beard work and lineups, every appointment is treated like a collaboration — you bring the vision, your barber brings the detail.

Booking stays simple on purpose: pick your barber, pick your service, book through Booksy, and pull up. No extra noise.
